Helpful Habits:

  1. Read good books:

    2. Audio books and high quality podcasts are included in this. The main objective of this is to expose yourself to new high quality ideas and knowledge.

  2. Do not watch too much TV:

    3. Unfortunately this includes YouTube and social media, though I would exclude video tutorials form this.

  3. Invest do not gamble:

    4. This does not mean this not taking risks with your money but instead to invest in capital.

  4. Exercise and eating well:

    5. When in shops just do not buy unhealthy food and even walking is exercise.

  5. Sleep well & getting up early:

    6. Keep your alarm constant. Take naps as required.

  6. Have multiple streams of income:

    7. Try to keep these streams independent of each other.

  7. Overwhelming positivity:

    8. This is easiest when you have a positive framework to view the world with.

  8. Have good manners:

    9. People are generally more receptive towards good manners, so swear less.

  9. Seek feedback to improve:

    10. Get feedback from people especially if it is negative, in fact not spirit.

  10. Work longer hours:

    11. But stay productive, sitting around is not going to produce anything.

  11. Avoid negativity & volunteer:

    12. Positivity generally builds more than negativity.

    You feel better about yourself and society after volunteering.

  12. Help others to succeed:

    13. Help, do not do everything.

  13. Set goals:

    14. Set goals or at minimum a broad general direction to move towards.
